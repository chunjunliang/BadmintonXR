Badminton Battle Update History

Version 1.1, updated on July 22, 2023

1. Add two small-area play modes in multiplayer action. This enables to play multiplayer match in small indoor area.

Version 1.0, updated on July 12, 2023

1. Fix the bug on racket selection.

2. Improve the badminton physics for keeping the shuttlecock on the racket surface when the relative speed of the racket and the shuttlecock is less than 1.5 m/s.

3. Adjust the badminton bonceness and the thickness of the net collider to reduce the chance of the shuttlecock passthrough when it hit the net with high speed.

4. Add region (China) selection for multiplayer mode.

5. Optimize the racket collider velocity calculation method from 3-point average to 2-point average.
