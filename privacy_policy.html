Badminton Battle Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which we collect, use, maintain, and disclose information collected from users (referred to as "you" or "user") of our VR badminton multiplayer game (referred to as "the Game") available on the Meta Quest store.

Information Collection and Use

We may collect personal identification information from you in various ways when you interact with the Game, including, but not limited to, when you register an account, make in-app purchases, participate in multiplayer sessions, or contact our customer support. The information we may collect includes, but is not limited to: • Your name • Email address • VR device information

We may also collect non-personal identification information about your interactions with the Game. This information may include technical details such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and gameplay statistics.
